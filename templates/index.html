Detectable Eye Diseases

Our system screens for four major eye conditions using retinal imaging

Cataract

Clouding of the eye's natural lens. Most common cause of vision loss in people over 40.

Moderate Urgency
Diabetic Retinopathy

Damage to blood vessels in the retina caused by diabetes. Leading cause of blindness.

High Urgency
Glaucoma

Optic nerve damage often caused by elevated intraocular pressure. Can cause blindness.

High Urgency
Normal

No disease indicators detected. Healthy retinal appearance with no abnormalities.
